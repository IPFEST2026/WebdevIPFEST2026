Geothermal Development Plan Competition

About

Get to Know More

The IPFEST 2026 Geothermal Development Plan Competition is a competition that challenges participants to develop and enhance their knowledge in renewable energy industry especially in creating the best solutions for a geothermal field development by analyzing the technical and nontechnical aspects (geological, geophysical, reservoir, economical, and HSE) of the field.
