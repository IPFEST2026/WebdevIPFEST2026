Hackathon Competition Division

About

Get to Know More

Hackathon Competition is an innovation-driven and team-based competition that invites undergraduate students from universities and institutions across Indonesia and abroad to solve real-world problems that are directly influenced by the critical needs of the energy and oil and gas industry. It will focus on key domains such as Reservoir & Production Optimization, Subsurface Resource Exploration, Asset Performance Analytics, and Drilling & Geomechanics, where the competition encourages the development of practical and scalable digitalized analytical solutions. The Hackathon Competition promotes a collaborative space for the participants to engage with industry experts and mentors and gain insight that bridges academic knowledge with real-world applications. It will also serve as a platform for them to showcase their innovative ideas that support the energy and petroleum industry toward a digital transformation and contribute to the global energy transition.
