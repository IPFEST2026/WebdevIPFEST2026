Paper & Poster Competition

About

Get to Know More

Paper and Poster Competition is a competition that challenges participants to conduct scientific research to create high-quality papers with strong writing and analytical skills related to the energy industry, especially in the oil and gas industry. This also challenges participants to do creative and innovative skills on a poster (represent the paper). The poster will be presented in the upcoming event of IPCONVEX 2026. The competition will be divided into preliminary (abstract submission) and final round (paper and poster submission, final presentation, and poster exhibition). At the preliminary round, delegates were required to write an abstract of the paper with the specified format. This abstract would be presented in accordance with the Paper and Poster Competition IPFEST 2026 theme. There will be the top ten teams who will qualify for the final round. Ten teams that qualified for the final round are required to complete the paper and create a poster and presentation. At the D-Day Competition, the ten teams will be required to present the paper that has already been submitted, using a PowerPoint presentation. Also, the ten teams will be required to exhibit their poster in IPEXPO IPFEST 2026.
