IPCONVEX

About

Get to Know More

IPFEST 2025 Integrated Petroleum Convention (IPCONVEX) is an integrated event with the concept of a grand seminar that will discuss topics in the oil and gas industry with emerging speakers from companies, governments, and academia. This is a place to maximize synergy within the energy stakeholders. This event also includes an exhibition called Competition Expo that displays the results of oil rig design competition and paper and poster competitions to be voted as favorite winners.

IPCONVEX

IPGL

The IPGL program offers a unique opportunity for students to deepen their understanding of the energy industry through a series of expertly curated lectures. These sessions are designed to go beyond traditional classroom education, providing fresh insights and covering topics that are often missed in formal curricula. Participants also get the invaluable chance to engage directly with seasoned engineers, allowing them to ask questions, gain practical perspectives, and build connections with professionals who are shaping the future of energy.

IPCONVEX

Career Talk

Career Talk is a tailored event that offers valuable insights for anyone aspiring to build a successful career, especially in the energy sector. Through this event, participants gain direct access to industry experts, including HR professionals, who share essential tips on how to stand out in a competitive job market. Attendees will learn the ins and outs of CV screening, what recruiters look for, and best practices for interviews—all crucial knowledge to enhance their employability and confidently navigate the recruitment process in the energy industry.

IPCONVEX

Grand IPCONVEX

The Grand Seminar of IPFEST 2025 is the highlight event, bringing together distinguished speakers, including top executives from leading energy companies and influential national stakeholders, to discuss the most pressing topics aligned with this year’s theme. This is your chance to gain invaluable insights from industry leaders and experience the pinnacle seminar of IPFEST 2025. Dive deep into the latest trends, innovations, and challenges shaping the future of the energy industry, and be inspired by the perspectives of those at the forefront of change. Don’t miss this unparalleled opportunity to elevate your understanding and connect with pioneers in the field.
