Is there a maximum and minimum number of team members for the IPFEST 2025 competition?

Each competition has different requirements for the number of team members: SC, MIC, and PPC require 2-3 members, BCC requires exactly 3 members, GDPC requires 4 members, and PODC, ORDC, and WDC require 4-5 members.

Is there a maximum number of competitions you can participate in?

Each participant is only allowed to register for one competition.

Are there any requirements to participate in the IPFEST 2025 competition?

Requirements such as an ID card or student card can be viewed later through the registration link.

Are there any specific majors required to participate in the IPFEST 2025 competition?

There are no major restrictions. All majors are welcome to participate.

Are there any specific qualifications required to participate in the IPFEST 2025 competition?

The competition is open to both national and international participants, but all participants must be enrolled in a Bachelor's degree program.

PT Pertamina Hulu Rokan (PHR) is a company that acts as an operator in managing the Rokan Working Area (WK), starting from 9 August 2021 to 8 August 2041. Production from the Rokan WK represents a quarter of Indonesia's crude oil production and is a snapshot of Pertamina's total production, 100% of which is processed by Pertamina's refineries.

With the support of more than two thousand workers and 38,000 contractors, WK Rokan is the largest onshore upstream oil and gas operation in Indonesia. The operational area is approximately 6,200 km2 covering 7 districts/cities in Riau Province. WK Rokan's first production began in 1952 and currently has more than 80 active fields with more than 12,000 wells. There are 35 collecting stations, 13,200 km of pipeline networks and 500 km of shipping networks that support the distribution of crude oil production.

The Rokan WK is the most productive oil block in Indonesia's oil history and makes a significant contribution to supporting national energy security and the country's economy. PHR contributed more than IDR 115 trillion in state revenue through PNBP and taxes since it was transferred to management in 2021.

Apart from contributing to providing energy for the nation's economic development, PHR is also committed to providing as many benefits as possible for the economic and social development of the Indonesian people, especially the people living around the operational area in Riau through various Social and Environmental Responsibility (TJSL) programs. The TJSL PHR program consists of 4 pillars: (1) Education, (2) Health, (3) Economic Empowerment, (4) Environment and disaster relief
